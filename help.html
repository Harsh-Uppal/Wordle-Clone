Wordle - Help

Goal of the game

A random word is selected from the words list. You have to guess that word to win. To guess a word, just type it in the grid and press enter.

How to Play

Type a 5 letter word in the grid and press Enter.

After pressing enter, R just turned yellow. It means that 'R' is in the word but not at the 4th position. If any letter turns green, it means that the letter is in the word and at the same positon. The letters that do not turn green or yellow they are not in the word. Using all the clues, try to guess the word in the least attempts.
